Untuk pelajaran Bahasa Mandarin, kami harus membuat sebuah esai tentang teman baru di St. Dominic Catholic High School, Kaohsiung, Taiwan R.O.C..


Ketentuan esainya adalah sebagai berikut (untuk Taiwan):

1. Topic: Describe your buddy

2. Make an essay about your buddies.

3. Type the essay, a minimum of 100 hanzi.

4. Font Recommendation: 字由点字青花楷 (Canva)

5. Attach photos related to the content (min. 3 photos, you must include your photos with your buddy).

6. Decorate it creatively.

7. Make the essay by yourself, please consider the grammatical and linguistic errors while using any website translation.
